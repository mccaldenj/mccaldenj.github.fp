Key Areas

1. Systems Design, Engineering, and Organization

An effective system needs to be organized to prioritize company needs. The interface should focus user attention on subscriptions, as they are a primary revenue source. Subscriptions should be convenient and require only necessary information. Convenience is key, as customers appreciate features like automatic refills and reduced effort in repurchasing.

2. Content and Information Architecture

Content should be simple and direct, with detailed information reserved for secondary pages. A clean homepage acts as the hierarchy of the website, enabling users to quickly navigate to desired information without feeling overwhelmed.

3. User Experience and Visual Design

Visual design elements such as pictures, graphics, large text, and color should direct attention to key topics. For example, the subscription button should be large, distinct, and easily noticeable. Familiarity in design fosters comfort for first-time visitors.

4. Avoiding Misinformation

Our website will prioritize factual accuracy and uphold company values through thorough fact-checking to eliminate misinformation. Ensuring credibility builds trust and user retention.
